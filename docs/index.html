Features

Everything you need to
recover with confidence.

CuraPath takes the confusion out of going home from the hospital — turning dense medical instructions into a simple, personalized recovery plan.

🤖
AI-Powered Parsing
Upload a photo or PDF of your discharge paperwork. Our AI extracts medications, restrictions, follow-up appointments, and warning signs instantly.
💊
Medication Reminders
Time-sensitive push notifications remind you to take each medication at the exact times listed on your discharge paperwork — even through Focus mode.
🩺
Daily Symptom Check-Ins
Answer a quick daily check-in based on the red flags in your specific discharge instructions. Know when something needs attention.
📋
Activity Restrictions
See your activity restrictions clearly laid out each day — no more hunting through paperwork to remember what you can and can't do.
📞
Provider Contact
Your care team's phone number is automatically extracted and saved so you can reach them instantly if you have concerns.
🔒
HIPAA Compliant
Built on AWS with encryption at rest and in transit, signed BAA, and secure JWT authentication. Your health data stays yours.
